About

A hub for chess players of IISER-Kolkata, the premier research institute in India.

The primary vision of the club is to act as a platform for players to sharpen their skills in the game, increase their critical thinking and to promote chess in the institute and beyond.

LEARN

We welcome beginners as we teach the basics of the game, discuss strategies and openings.

PRACTICE

The harder you practice, the luckier you get.
Join us as we play matches, analyze games and solve puzzles.

COMPETE

Showcase your practice and intelligence against the best as we conduct tournaments and open events.

Events

Institute Events

Discover the illustrious customary events conducted by us in the institute

Grand Chess Tournament

The most prestigious chess tournament at IISER-K, with participants from the entire institute, battling for a position against the best of the best.


Format: Classical

System: Round Robin

Rating Matches

Rating Matches continue to be the most popular event of all time. Players with similar ratings are matched together as they play rapid games in a swiss system.


Format: Rapid

System: Swiss

Inter-Batch Series

IISER-K quite fancies the Inter-Batch Series. Students of a batch come together to contend against other batches to claim the title of winners.


Format: Rapid

System: Single-Elimination

Blitz Chess

Introduced recently, blitz chess creates a flurry of moves, as both players attempt to hustle to victory. Quite enjoyed by folks who love to experience a blazing fast game and even faster thinking.


Format: Blitz

System: Swiss

Open Events

Get to know the finest and quintessential open chess events hosted by Chess Club of IISER-K

Inquivesta 9 Open

In Association with Bengal Chess Association(BCA)

Inquivesta Open prides in being one of the biggest and commemorated open chess tournaments of all time in Kolkata. This edition, the chess club collaborated with the Bengal Chess Associationa and the event was a remarkable success with a footfall of over 100 people.


Format: Classical

System: Round Robin
